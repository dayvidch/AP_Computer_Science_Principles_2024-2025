# AP Computer Science Principles 2024 - 2025

I took AP CSP my senior year of high school. I have never taken an AP class prior to AP CSP. 
I decided to take this class my senior year just to be able to say that I did in fact take an AP class during my time in high school.
This class was taken at Dublin High School during the 2024-2025 academic year.
To be honest, this class was pretty shit. The teacher was pretty shit too. In fact, the teacher didn't even know how to code themself. 
One time, during a test, my school computer did not work properly and the teacher literally sat there and looked at the screen for about 20 minutes trying to fix it.
At the end, they couldn't fix the issue and said that there was nothing that they could do and I just had to figure it out. 
Thanks teacher, thank you for wasting 20 out of 60 minutes of my testing time limit and giving me a faulty computer.
There were no live lessons, the teacher would just assign textbook problems throughout the whole year.
It pretty much felt like busy work and a big waste of time.
The only one benefit that I did gain from this class was that I got a lot stronger in my ability to work with bigger pieces of code just by the sheer amount of code we had to do.
